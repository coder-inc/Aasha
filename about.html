About Us

Discover Aasha and its Benefits

The way we borrow money and the way we invest money is changing. Back in the day, if you wanted a loan to pay off your car or credit cards, you’d go to a bank or a credit union, sit down with a loan officer, and wait for them to tell you yes or no as they “crunched the numbers.” Investing was always done with a traditional broker – online or in-person.

AASHA is a platform for social investing. AASHA is India’s trusted peer-to-peer lending platform that enables individuals to invest in entrepreneurs from low-income households helping them build sustainable livelihoods. That's how we stand apart from other lending platforms. But times have changed. Instead of going to banks and brokers, we simply go to each other. That’s exactly what peer-to-peer lending is all about – helping each other, rather than relying on big banks.

That’s why we have created Aasha as a marketplace where people could lend money to one another. It benefits both sides, too. Investees gets money without having to go through a bank, and investers can make money by loaning their funds to other people.

100% of the investor's capital goes straight to the investee. You choose who you want to invest in and we keep you updated on their progress. Aasha is a totally secured platform where we commit to maintain confidentiality, integrity and security of your personal information.
